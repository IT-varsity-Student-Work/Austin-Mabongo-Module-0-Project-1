LONDON,ENGLAND

London is the capital city of one of the famous nations of Great Britain and is a popular tourist destination.Besides football many people travel to the city for various other reasons like the tourist attractions around the city.It is also the home of the royal family.London is know also for its heritage sites and its famous landmarks

Must visit locations incude...

*London Eye

Also known as the Millennium Wheel,the London Eye is a observation wheel located at the South bank of the River Thomas wheel in around the city of London.It is well known as the most popular paid tourist attraction in the UK with millions visiting annually with over 3.75 million visitors yearly.The London eye used to offer public viewing in London to see the city while enjoying a 360 degress joy ride.One can take breathtaking photos from the London eye to gloat after a visit to the city

*Buckingham Place (Royal Residence)

Internationally known as the resident headquarters of the monarchy of the UK(United Kingdom) ,the palace palace is a great site and attracrts tourist from all over even England.The infrastructure and the gardens around the palace makes it so pleasing to the eye.Queen Elizabeth currently is the queen in the palace.It is located in the city of Westminster.The palace is the centre of state and royal occasions.The palace is a symbol of the British monarchy,an art gallery and a major tourist attraction.

*Big Ben

Big Ben now known as Elizabeth Tower since 2012 to mark the Diamond Jubilee of Elizabeth II,is the great Bell of the striking clock at the north end of Westminster palace.The tower was designed by Augustus Pugin.The clock is a cultural symbol of the UK,it is recognised worldwide especially by foreign visitors.The interior of the Tower is not open to overseas visitors.

*Trafalgar Square

Trafalgar square is a public square in Westminster,central london.The name represents the battle of Trafalgar.The square has been used for community gatherings and also political activities.The square is a "THE LOCATION" for New Years Eve celebrations.If one visits during that time one can make new people and socialise with locals to get a better understanding of the city of London.
