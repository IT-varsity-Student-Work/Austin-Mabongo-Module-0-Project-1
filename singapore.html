SINGAPORE

Singapore is a sovereign island city-state with rich cultural diversity and beautiful central business district infrastructure like the 3 building with a ship on top.It has a number of tourist attractions in the city and one can choose where to go.The different cultures,scenery and the people,food and night life has attracted people from all over the planet.Singapore got 17.4 million international visits in 2017,which is three times its population.It is also environmentally friendly and maintain natural and heritage conservation programmes

Notable atractions include:

*Orchard Road District

Orchard Road is Singapore’s is main road, with discount outlets, department stores and upscale boutiques, alongside luxury hotels. Emerald Hill Road is a mix of colorful Chinese baroque houses and casual pubs, while al fresco bars and eateries can be found along nearby Cuppage Terrace. The Newton Food Centre is a popular foodie hub, offering everything from barbecued seafood and roasted meats to traditional desserts.

*Night Safari

It is the worlds first nectural zoo and is visited very frequently yearly.The night safari currently holds 900 animals representing over 100 spcies.The Night Safari is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Singapore.

*River Safari

It is a river-themed zoo and aquarime.It is the first of its kinf in Asia and features freshwater exhibits and river boat ride as its main highlights.The park was officially opened on 28 February 2014, and it was announced that more than 1.1 million have visited the River Safari since its soft opening in April 2013.The park consists of a total of 10 different ecosystems around the world, including the River Nile, Ganges River, Yangtze River, Mississippi, Amazon as well as the Tundra. It features 5000 animals of 300 species, including numerous that are endangered. Among these are anacondas, electric eels, Mekong freshwater stingrays, West Indian manatees, Mekong giant catfish, giant pandas, gharials, red pandas, golden pheasants, paddlefish, jaguars, squirrel monkeys, giant salamanders, giant otters and Chinese alligators that are part of an exchange program with the Asahiyama Zoo in Hokkaidō, Japan.It is a one in a million experiences so dont miss out
